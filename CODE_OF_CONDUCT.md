# Code of Conduct

We aim for clear, respectful, and productive collaboration.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and concise
- Critique ideas, not people
- Keep discussions actionable

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment or hate speech
- Spam, doxxing, threats
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement
Maintainers may edit/remove content or block contributors violating these rules.
